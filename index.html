About Me

1995

Hi! My name is João Amaral, and i’m a front end developer, based in Lisbon.
I’ve Graduated at ETIC’s Web Responsive course between 2018 - 2019, and since then, i’ve been immersed in the front end side of it.

Actually i’ve been focusing in Javascript/ES6, alongside SASS/SCSS, HTML5 and CSS3, but also have the desire of extending the knowledge into a more back end side in the future.

As a developer, i believe that developing is the most creative way of solving and debugging the main aspects of design, because of it’s diversity and oneness.

When i dont work, im a guy who likes to spend time cooking new things, watching football, discovering music and new places as well to visit.
